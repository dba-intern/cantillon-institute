HOW TO CITE
OUR WORK

Full bibliographic entries for all working papers published by The Cantillon Institute, formatted in APA style. All papers are available as PDFs at cantillon.institute.

Standard Format

Author, I. (Year). Title of paper (Working Paper No. X). The Cantillon Institute. https://cantillon.institute/papers/filename.pdf
